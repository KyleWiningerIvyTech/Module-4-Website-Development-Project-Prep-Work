ENDING ON A HIGH NOTE



Sly 3 Honor Among Thieves was released in September 26, 2005.



Thanks to how well Sly 2 performed, the dev team had much more confidence going in to Sly 3. Instead of trying to survive as a studio, they could instead go all out with Sly 3, filling it with all kinds of mini games and extra polish. Fans and critics welcomed the extra variety to the gameplay in this final entry.



Sly 3 once again sold around 1.2 million copies, recapturing the glory of Sly 2 and allowing Suckerpunch to confidently leave the series behind and move on to a new IP. Despite being done with the franchise however, they left a small cliffhanger in Sly 3 so that another developer could take the reins, if given the chance.
