ADAPTING TO THE TIMES





Sly 2 retained a lot of what made Sly 1 memorable. What is new in Sly 2 is its more open structure. The developers drew inspiration from Disneyland with the world design, allowing the player to complete jobs that build towards a big heist at the end of each episode. These changes were welcomed by fans and critics alike.

Sly 2 Band of Thieves was released in September 14, 2004.

Sly 2 was a step up from Sly 1 in the sales department, selling around 1.2 million copies and providing Suckerpunch the necessary funds to stay afloat for the forseeable future. With this success, Suckerpunch would begin development on one last Sly game. Only this time, it would be a more enjoyable challenge for the team.
